Teacher Resources

These resources were chosen to support real classroom needs, whether that’s strengthening literacy skills, finding diverse and inclusive texts, or bringing meaningful technology into instruction. They’re practical, easy to use, cost effective, and flexible enough to fit a variety of grade levels and teaching styles. My goal is to gather tools that help teachers save time, spark ideas, and create learning experiences that connect with all students.
