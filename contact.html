Visit McNabb Middle School

J. B. McNabb Middle School is located in Mount Sterling, Kentucky, serving around 950 students. The school supports a diverse student population and offers a wide range of academic, arts, and extracurricular opportunities to meet different interests and needs. With a strong commitment to the arts, McNabb Middle promotes creativity through programs in music, visual arts, and performance experiences. This focus helps students develop confidence, expression, and collaboration alongside their academic growth.
